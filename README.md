# 🧠 SoulSync – The Intelligent Diary Companion

SoulSync is an AI-powered emotional wellness and journaling application designed to help users express their thoughts, understand their emotions, and build healthy self-reflection habits. By combining intelligent conversations, emotional insights, and multimedia journaling, SoulSync creates a safe and supportive digital space for users to document their experiences and promote emotional well-being.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📝 Intelligent Journaling

* Create and manage diary entries with rich text support.
* Edit and revisit previous journal entries.
* Organize entries chronologically for easy access.

### 🤖 AI Chat Companion

* Engage in natural conversations with an AI-powered companion.
* Receive empathetic, context-aware responses.
* Maintain short-term conversational memory for meaningful interactions.

### 📄 AI-Powered Journal Summarization

* Convert chat conversations into structured first-person diary entries.
* Preserve emotional context and important details.
* Edit generated summaries before saving them.

### 😊 Emotion-Aware Reflection

* Analyze diary entries to understand emotional context.
* Receive personalized reflections, encouragement, and supportive feedback.
* Promote self-awareness and emotional growth.

### 📸 Rich Multimedia Journaling

Enhance diary entries with:

* Images
* Audio recordings
* Videos
* Hand-drawn sketches
* Stickers

### 📅 Calendar & Mood Tracking

* View journaling activity by date.
* Track emotional patterns over time.
* Access mood insights and emotional trends.

### 👤 Profile & Personalization

* Customize profile information.
* View mood statistics and summaries.
* Personalize the app experience with theme preferences.

### 🔔 Productivity Features

* Built-in to-do list.
* Reminder notifications for journaling habits and important tasks.
* Encourage consistency and organization.

### 🔒 Privacy & Security

* Secure authentication.
* App lock functionality for protecting sensitive content.
* Cloud synchronization for reliable data storage.

## 🎯 Project Goal

SoulSync aims to simplify emotional expression and encourage consistent self-reflection by combining journaling, artificial intelligence, and productivity features into a single intuitive mobile application. The platform serves as a supportive companion, helping users better understand their emotions while promoting emotional balance and well-being.
